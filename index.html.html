Real Telemetry via OpenF1
OpenF1 API delivers real F1 car positions, brake signatures, speed traces, GPS coordinates, and delta times at incident-frame precision. FastF1 provides historical race data 2018-2026. Free, zero authentication required.
📚
IBM Docling RAG
IBM Docling ingests 500+ FIA steward decision PDFs (2010-2026), Sporting Regulations, Technical Regulations, and 2025/2026 Driving Standards Guidelines into FAISS. Every Granite call is regulation-grounded -- zero hallucination on regulation text.
🧠
IBM Granite Reasoning
IBM Granite 3.1-8B-Instruct receives computed telemetry features + retrieved regulation passages and generates structured steward briefs: breach assessment, severity 0-10, precedent match, penalty recommendation, and confidence level.
⚖️
Steward Decision Brief
Structured 60-second brief for FIA stewards: incident summary, articles breached, top precedents, severity score, recommended penalty range, and full audit trail. Built for the 4-minute steward decision window in live racing.
🏎️
Team Appeal Intelligence
Teams enter counter-arguments. GridLock AI scores appeal strength against 500+ historical FIA decisions via IBM Docling RAG, generating a structured submission brief. Equal access to legal intelligence regardless of team budget.
📺
Fan Explainability Layer
Plain-language explanation for 500M+ fans -- what happened, which rule was broken, why that penalty, and the closest historical parallel. Generated in under 30 seconds. Real F1, zero jargon. Clarity for every fan globally.
